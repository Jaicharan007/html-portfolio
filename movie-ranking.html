The Best Movies According to Jai Charan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Surya s/o.Krishnan

This indian based on ideal realtionship between a father and a son.A perfect way to how to deal with failures in life.A romatic love story and my forever favourite music album.

Jersey

This is a story of a facinated indian father who was likes to play cricket for indian cricket team but didnt be able to continue and becomes unemployed.Love of his life also slowly starts hating for his unployement.for his incapability of fulfilling his son's wish for his birthday he again start to chose to play cricket at the age of 36.climax would make you feel emotional.must watch film (in telugu version).

Colour Photo

this is a telugu film based on a love story between a dark skined boy and a fair girl and their problems in the girls house. this is an beautiful movie which makes you cry at the climax.brilliant details in the story in background which makes me like it more and more each time i watch this film.
